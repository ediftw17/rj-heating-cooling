Our Story

Family-Owned Since 1946

R.J. Heating & Cooling has been keeping Cleveland homes comfortable for over 80 years. What started as a neighborhood service company has grown into one of the West Side’s most trusted names in heating and cooling, without losing the personal touch that made us who we are.

Under the ownership of Charles Dolph, we’ve stayed true to the founding values: honest diagnosis, fair pricing, and workmanship that speaks for itself. We’ve built relationships across generations of Cleveland families, and many of our customers today are the children and grandchildren of our original clients.

As a Bryant Authorized Dealer, we back our service with manufacturer-approved parts and certified training. When you call R.J., you’re not calling a call center. You’re calling your neighbors.
